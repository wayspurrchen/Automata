automata

Conway's Game of Life, but with violence

Helpful tips:

  • Cells either move to or divide into the nearest (empty) space
  • Cells will "fight" with nearby individual enemy cells
  • Cells surrounded by three or more cells belonging to the same opponent are consumed
  • You can click on the canvas to add cells
  • Click the outlined square to change the color of the cells you place
